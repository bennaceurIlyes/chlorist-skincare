The Transformation

From Research to Rituals

The story of a biotechnology research project turned mission: transforming academic science into real dermatological solutions.

Chapter I

The Spark in the Lab

It all started on university laboratory benches. What was initially just a Master's project in biotechnology and cosmetic formulation quickly became evident: science can do better.

While exploring skin biology mechanisms, we discovered the untapped potential of Algerian natural resources, often ignored by mass industry. Our journey began with a simple question: how to combine research rigor with the purity of the earth?

Science-Backed

Each Chlorice formula is the result of months of research, tested for effectiveness and stability.

Desert Soul

We draw inspiration from the resilience of Algerian desert flora, capable of surviving the most extreme conditions.

Sustainable

Respect for skin and the environment is at the heart of our approach, from source to packaging.

Where Biology Meets Beauty

At Chlorice, we integrate molecular biology and formulation engineering to create products that don't just mask problems, but treat them at the root.

Our approach combines cutting-edge biotechnological actives with highly dosed botanical extracts, creating a unique synergy that respects your skin's ecology.

Heritage

Proudly From Bechar

Chlorice is a deeply Algerian brand. Inspired by the natural riches of our desert and driven by the passion of its entrepreneurial youth.

We believe in transforming local resources into high-value-added products, proving that Algeria can be synonymous with luxury, science, and innovation in the world of cosmetics.

The Future of Chlorice

Our ambition is to continue pushing the boundaries of skincare biotechnology, developing new patented formulas and representing Algerian excellence on an international scale.
